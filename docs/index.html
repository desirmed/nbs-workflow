Application of the Conceptual Framework: Step-by-Step Guide

This guide will walk you through a step-by-step workflow for creating and implementing NbS portfolios tailored to various regions. We provide a structured guideline to identify opportunities and design interventions using nature-based solutions for your area of interest. The workflow outlined here helps you to understand key factors like risks, ecosystem services, and socio-economic and ecological dynamics, enabling a science-driven and place-based approach towards community resilience.
