This guide aims to walk you through a step-by-step workflow for creating and implementing NbS portfolios tailored to various regions. We provide a structured pathway to identify opportunities and design interventions using nature. The workflow outlined here helps us understand key factors like flood exposure, ecosystem connectivity, and demographic dynamics, enabling a science-driven approach to planning. You’ll explore how integrating data from geospatial and modelling platforms facilitates efficient analysis and meaningful insights.
